      HOW TO PLAY

      • Pick a category — TV, Games, or Sports. You get 5 rounds from that theme.
      • Each image starts pixelated and slowly clears over 15 seconds (watch the bar).
      • Type your guess below the picture and tap SUBMIT or press Enter. Wrong guesses don’t end the round.
      • Faster correct answers score more (up to 1000 points per round). Waiting until the end still earns a small minimum if you get it right.
      • Pass skips the round for 0 points. If you pass or time runs out, the answer is shown.
      • After 5 rounds, save your name to the local and global leaderboards.
